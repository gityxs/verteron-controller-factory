Your goal is to build 1 million Verteron Controllers. In order to do that, you first have to build their ingridients: Assembly Circuits, Phase Buffers and Multitronic Arrays. Initially everything is slow and inefficient. By upgrading your technological stack, you can turn your factory into a wonder machine of unbelievable capacity!

You have only 30 minutes to complete the game, so choose your strategy wisely!
Factory value will be paid out to you each time the amount of cycles becomes 0.

Cycles are reduced each time a unit is built and can also be reduced by using the manual button.

Buying a Rubidium Compensator    will grow the value of the factory with each Verteron Controller percent. However, a Verteron Controller build will reduce the effect by a small amount.

A Verteron Controller is the end product of the factory.

If you buy a Rubidium Compensator    upgrade, every percent of a Verteron Controller will grow the value of the factory.

Each time a new Verteron Controller is built, the amount of Multitronic Arrays required for the next one goes up by 1. So, initially 2 Multitronic Arrays are required for each percent of a Verteron Controller. When it is built, 3 Multitronic Arrays will be required for each percent of the next one, and so on.
